The Lion of the Desert Club Charter 2023

1 The group of people herein describes as an investment club.

a) Shall thus be subjected to the laws of the land upon which it is duly registered.

a) The club is comprised of 10 members and 7/10 or more make up quorum:

i) - The number of memberships is not limited.
ii) - A formal request shall be presented by the endorsing member(Already registered) and it shall be subject to vetting by club members before submission of a new entrant.

2 All members are required to present their credentials:
i) Passport copy/photo or Qatar Residence Permit(QID) Copy.
ii) One of the above for the next of kin.
iii) A close up picture and Their physical & Digital address(emails & Phone number.. etc).

3 Respect for either members' religion, culture and Opinion; Members shall refrain from:
i) The use of profanity and use of foul language to ridicule another's opinion or in any form.
ii) Politically motivated statements.
iii) Any kind of blasphemous utterances or draft policies which are taboo.

4 An amount of 500 Qatari riyals as a contribution from every member for atleast a period of 10 months(Or 5,000qrs lumpsum) but not less, shall be considered as every member's minimum initial share/Capital:
a) All funds shall be submitted to the treasurer for safe keeping and accountability.
i) The treasurer has to present a financial statement(Document/Screenshot evidence) to the Club's platform every after one month.
b) A member shall only have a one month waiver towards contribution and shall give prior communication to the treasurer if faced with emergency challenges.
i) A member shall contribute 1000qrs to make up for the previous exempted month.[See article 4(b)]
ii) Any member going for vacation shall give formal communication to the treasurer and will have to provide funds for two months upfront.

5 A member who is faced with severe health challenges is exempted from contributing towards the club's fund for that specific period of time but shall make up for it in the subsequent time.

6 Any member who wishes to withdraw from the club shall:
i) Provide a notice of intent to terminate membership 6 months prior.
ii) Shall be charged 10% from his/her share & benefits accumulated as a levy for the inconveniences caused to the club.
iii) Shall give number one priority to club members upon a decision to sale off his/her shares.
iv) Shall only sale shares externally if all club members fail or show no interest in buying the shares.

7 A Club membership shall be terminated in a case of gross violation of charter rules or as decided unanimously by the club members(if quorum is met):
a) The Club's internal affairs shall be kept private, only within the membership circles unless if officially sanctioned and limited to only;
i) The Club's business or in sought of corporate endorsements from various organisations.
ii) A member shall not conduct business on behalf of the club unless fully sanctioned to perform such a task.
iii) Articles 6(ii), 6(iii), 6(iv) shall be invoked upon the process of termination of membership.

8 Upon the unfortunate demise of a member, their share/capital & benefits shall be passed unto his next of kin with no charges subjected:
i) Who shall thus decide to further with membership of the club or not.
ii) Give club members priority to buy off the shares before floating them externally.

9 All the above articles herein are subject to amendment to the utmost satisfaction of the club members for as long as quorum is met and the changes are thus duly effected.
